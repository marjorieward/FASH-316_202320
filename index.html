Class One - Prepping for the Basic Five

✕

Before Class:

  • Complete the Pre-Quarter Assignment
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Ticonderonga #2 Pencil
    • Pencil sharpener
    • Tape measure
    • 1" small silk pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • 3-5 yards black ¼ twill tape
    • 12 inches white ½ twill tape

During Class:

  • Applying HBLs and control lines to the dress form.
  • Prepping muslin fabrics for draping assignments.

After Class:

  • Students will apply HBLs to the dress form and prepare fabric for Project 1: The Basic Five Drape.
  • Prepare four pieces of medium-weight muslin by doing the following:
    • Measure the dress form properly.
    • Snip and rip muslin to the appropriate size.
    • Properly label the muslin with your name, piece name, grainline, and dressform ID.
    • Properly block the muslin and mark grainlines in preparation for accurate draping.
    • Complete this process for the skirt front, skirt back, bodice front, and bodice back muslin pieces.
